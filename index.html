About Us

Founded and operated by M.S.M. Nawshard in Ratnapura, Sri Lanka, Nawshard Gems specializes in trading blue sapphires, rubies, emeralds, fancy sapphires, and semi-precious stones. The company focuses on quality, authenticity, and long-standing relationships.

Trading actively in Sri Lanka, Madagascar, East African countries, and Bangkok, Nawshard Gems sources gemstones in both rough and cut & polished forms. Rough stones are often treated, cut, and polished to enhance their natural beauty and value. The company also deals in ready-to-sell stones, available as individual pieces or in selected lots, tailored to the needs of jewelers, collectors, and dealers.

With a solid foundation in key gemstone markets, Nawshard Gems is now working to expand its reach to new international markets, continuing its mission to trade exceptional gemstones with integrity and expertise.
