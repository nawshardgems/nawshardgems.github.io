About Us

Founded by M.S.M. Nawshard and based in Ratnapura, Sri Lanka — Nawshard Gems is engaged in the buying and selling of high-quality gemstones, particularly blue sapphires, rubies, fancy sapphires, and semi-precious stones.

We work with rough, cut, and polished stones — including heat-treated stones — and offer both individual pieces and curated lots. Nawshard Gems sources and trades gemstones primarily in Sri Lanka, Madagascar, East Africa, and Bangkok, with a growing interest in expanding to new international markets.

What We Offer

  • Blue Sapphires (rough and polished)
  • Rubies and other fancy sapphires
  • Heat-treated and natural stones
  • Semi-precious stones
  • Stone lots for wholesale buyers
